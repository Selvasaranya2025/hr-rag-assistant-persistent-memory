COMPANY HR POLICY MANUAL
Effective Date: January 2026

1. Employment Classification
Employees are classified as Full-Time, Part-Time, Contract, or Intern.
Full-Time employees work 40 hours per week and are eligible for all benefits.
Part-Time employees work less than 30 hours per week and receive limited benefits.
Contract employees are not eligible for company-sponsored benefits.

2. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Flexible work arrangements may be approved by the reporting manager.
Employees must log a minimum of 40 hours per week.

3. Leave Policy
3.1 Annual Leave
Full-Time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Unused leave up to 5 days may be carried forward to the next year.

3.2 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave annually.
Medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

3.3 Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
Maternity benefits apply only after completion of 12 months of continuous service.

3.4 Paternity Leave
Male employees are entitled to 10 days of paid paternity leave.

4. Work From Home Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with prior approval.
Extended remote work requires HR and department head approval.

5. Performance Evaluation
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually.
Promotions and salary increments are based on performance rating and business needs.

6. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, and workplace misconduct will result in disciplinary action.

7. Notice Period and Termination
Full-Time employees must serve a 60-day notice period.
The company reserves the right to provide payment in lieu of notice.
Immediate termination may occur in cases of serious misconduct.

8. Health Insurance
Full-Time employees are eligible for company-sponsored health insurance.
Coverage includes hospitalization, maternity, and emergency services.
Dependents may be added at additional premium cost.

9. Reimbursement Policy
Business travel and work-related expenses are reimbursable upon submission of valid receipts.
Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense occurrence.
